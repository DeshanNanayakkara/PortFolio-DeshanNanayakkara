Green Shadow - Crop Management System

Green Shadow is a crop management system designed to streamline farm operations. It allows farm managers to efficiently track field data, monitor crop health, allocate tasks to staff, and manage fields.


Key Features:


  • Manage field and crop details

  • Monitor crop growth and health

  • Allocate and manage staff tasks

  • Upload and organize field images

  • User-friendly interface for easy navigation
